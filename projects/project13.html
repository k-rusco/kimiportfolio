Green Unity

UI/UX Design (2025)

This project was part of my final assessment for my sducation in Interactive Media Design, before uni. We were reuired to develop an app, in which the city hall would give users the opportunity to choose how certain unused public green areas could be used in the future. The areas would be open for suggestions for 3 months, and users could propose any ideas. Afterwards, and after a short review period, the town hall-approved choises would be up for voting. The idea with the most votes would be chosen and implemented.
