150 Museum fur Gestaltung

Reactive Coding (2025)

In collaboration with the Zürcher Museum für Gestaltung, me and my team were tasked to create countdown for museum's 150th anniversary, that reacted to the movenets of the different passersby. The countdown was meant to be projected the 3 big windows right above the entrance. Me and my team decided to code this countdown in JavaScript, creating a dynamic flowy ASCII animation. The reactive part comes with this fog-like layer that inverts the base colors and follows the passersby.
