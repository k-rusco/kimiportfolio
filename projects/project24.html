A Bonk to the Past

Illustration - Character Design (2024)

Every year, an event called Global Game Jam takes place globally. This event is a global hackathon in which teams of devs, designers, and enthusiasts gather to develop a videogame, following a given theme in 48 hours. I've been participating since 2023 as an artist and ui designer. This particular year's theme was "roots". My team and I decided too interpret it by creating a game situated at the roots of humanity. In this game, you are a hunter/gatherer, trying to retrieve items for your fellow cave mates. I took care of the character, asset, and ui design.
